# 🩺 Health Advisor AI

### Your Personal AI-Powered Health Companion

> **🌐 Live App → [https://health--advisor.vercel.app/](https://health--advisor.vercel.app/)**

---

Health Advisor AI is a modern, intelligent health companion designed to help you understand your symptoms, explore possible conditions, find hospitals near you, and have an informed conversation about your health — all from your browser, instantly, with no sign-up required.

Built with a focus on privacy, speed, and accessibility, Health Advisor AI brings the power of advanced AI to your fingertips, whether you're on a phone, tablet, or desktop.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔬 AI-Powered Symptom Analysis
Describe how you're feeling in plain English and let the AI do the heavy lifting. Health Advisor analyzes your symptoms and returns a structured health report that includes:
- **Identified condition** — the most likely cause of your symptoms
- **Risk Level** — Low, Medium, or High, so you know how seriously to take it
- **Risk Score** — a percentage-based severity indicator
- **Recommended Action** — whether to rest at home, visit a clinic, or go to the emergency room
- **Specialist Recommendation** — which type of doctor would best address your condition

### 📋 Detailed Health Reports
Every symptom scan generates a full, printable health report. You can save reports to your personal history and review them any time. Reports are stored locally on your device — your data never leaves your browser.

### 🏥 Nearby Hospitals
Instantly find hospitals and medical facilities near your current location using real-time geolocation. Each result includes the hospital name, address, contact number, and a direct link to get directions via Google Maps.

### 🤖 AI Chat — Meet Dr. WHO
Have a conversation with Dr. WHO, your empathetic AI health companion. Ask follow-up questions about your symptoms, get general health advice, or simply talk through your concerns. Dr. WHO is designed to guide you — not replace a real doctor — and will always encourage you to seek professional care when needed.

### 📊 Personal Health History
Every saved report is organized chronologically in your History tab. View a **Monthly Health Overview** showing your 30-day scan count, average risk level, high-risk alerts, and the most common condition you've been flagged for. Drill down into individual records with full symptom breakdowns and specialist notes.

### 👤 Multi-User Profiles
Health Advisor supports multiple user profiles on the same device. Each person's data (name, age, gender, blood group, and full history) is stored separately under their profile. Switch between users anytime.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy First

Health Advisor AI is built with your privacy as a priority:

- **No account required** — there is no sign-up, login, or email verification
- **No cloud storage** — all your health data is stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`; it never leaves your device
- **No tracking** — we do not collect, sell, or share any personal or health data
- **Fully client-side** — the interface runs entirely in your browser

## ⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

Health Advisor AI is an **informational tool only**. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you read or received from this application.

In a medical emergency, **call your local emergency number immediately**.

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Create your profile** — Enter your name, age, gender, and blood group on first launch
2. **Describe your symptoms** — Type how you're feeling in the Diagnostic tab
3. **Get your report** — The AI analyzes your input and generates a structured health assessment
4. **Save to history** — Optionally save the report to your personal health history
5. **Explore more** — Find nearby hospitals, chat with Dr. WHO, or review past scans

## 🖥️ Technology

Health Advisor AI is built entirely with modern web technologies:

- **Frontend** — HTML5, Tailwind CSS, Vanilla JavaScript
- **AI Engine** — Google Gemini API (via serverless backend)
- **Maps & Location** — Browser Geolocation API + OpenStreetMap Overpass API
- **Storage** — Browser `localStorage` (fully private, no server)
- **Hosting** — Vercel (serverless, global edge network)
